# Brand

## Voice
- Confident but warm. Like a curator introducing a rare creature.
- No hype words. No "revolutionary", no "next-gen".
- Show, don't tell: let the creature's art do the work.

## Tone
- Cozy-meets-cosmic. "Living creature on a high-tech chain."
- A bit of awe — the hatch moment is sacred. Don't bury it in copy.

## Don'ts
- No "gm", no "wagmi", no "moon".
- No referral-program wording.
- No "limited time" urgency. MonadMon is a keepsake, not a drop.

## Tagline (final candidates)
- "The first living creatures on Monad."
- "My first Monad creature, born on-chain, mine forever."
- "Twelve species. One Monad. Yours."
